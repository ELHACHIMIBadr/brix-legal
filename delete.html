Delete Your BRIX Account

Last updated: June 14, 2026

You can delete your BRIX account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how to do it and what data will be removed.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the BRIX app on your Android device
  2. Tap the Profile tab (bottom right)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Alternatively, you can request account deletion by emailing us at privacy@brixapp.co with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and your registered email address.

What data gets deleted

Upon account deletion, the following data is permanently removed within 30 days:

  • Your portfolio (all sets, purchase prices, conditions, notes)
  • Your wishlist and price alerts
  • Your profile information (name, email, profile picture)
  • Your subscription status (managed via RevenueCat)

What data may be retained

The following data may be retained for a limited period after deletion:

  • Anonymized analytics data (Firebase Analytics) — retained for up to 14 months in aggregate, non-identifiable form
  • Crash reports (Firebase Crashlytics) — retained for up to 90 days
  • Purchase records — retained as required by applicable law (up to 7 years for financial records)
⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Your portfolio data cannot be recovered after deletion.
